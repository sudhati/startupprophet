Attributes of Startup Success Prediction

Understanding the key attributes used to predict the success of startups.

01)is_ecommerce

This attribute indicates whether the startup operates in the e-commerce sector.i.e. sellings goods or online service

02)is_otherstate

This attribute shows if the startup is based outside the primary startup hubs.

03)has_vc

This attribute signifies if the startup has received venture capital funding.

04)has_angel

This attribute indicates whether the startup has received funding from angel investors.

05)has_roundA

This attribute denotes if the startup has successfully raised a Series A funding round.i.e.did they have plan for developing bussiness.

06)has_roundB

This attribute indicates whether the startup has raised a Series B funding round.i.e.scaling the team members

07)has_roundC

This attribute signifies if the startup has completed a Series C funding round.i.e.develop new products,expand to new markets.

08)has_roundD

This attribute shows whether the startup has raised a Series D funding round.

09)is_top500

This attribute shows if the startup is ranked among the top 500 startups in its category or region.

10)funding_rounds

This attribute indicates the total number of funding rounds the startup has gone through.

11)milestones

This attribute denotes the number of significant milestones the startup has achieved, such as product launches, market expansions, or major partnerships.

12)relationships

This attribute signifies the number of key relationships and partnerships the startup has established, which can be crucial for its growth and success.
